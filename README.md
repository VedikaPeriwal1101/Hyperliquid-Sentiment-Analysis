## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes the psychological and execution patterns of Hyperliquid traders in relation to market sentiment (Fear & Greed Index). I processed over 210,000 trades to identify how "Smart Money" shifts behavior during market extremes.

## 🧠 Key Insights (Data-Backed)
1. **The Fear Profitability Peak:** Standard "Fear" days generated the highest aggregate PnL ($3.35M), proving that apex traders capitalize on panic-driven volatility.
2. **The Contrarian Shift:** During "Extreme Greed," the Long/Short ratio dropped to 0.81, indicating that top-tier traders bet against the trend when euphoria is at its peak.
3. **The Win-Rate Paradox:** 91% of the total profit was generated by traders with a win rate below 50%, highlighting the importance of Risk-to-Reward over simple accuracy.

## 📈 Actionable Strategies
* **Rule 1:** Increase position sizing by 50% during "Fear" regimes to capture asymmetric reversals.
* **Rule 2:** Enforce a "Short-only" bias during "Extreme Greed" to hedge against inevitable market corrections.
